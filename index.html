Joe Sutherland

is a journalist and communicator.

Hey there. My name's Joe Sutherland and I'm currently a communications volunteer at the Wikimedia Foundation. I'm a freelance writer for independent music blog Hercules Moments, and also blog about tech and video games sometimes.

Right now I'm studying journalism at the Robert Gordon University in Aberdeen, Scotland. I've spent the past year at Hogeschool Utrecht in the Netherlands, and the Danish School of Media and Journalism in Aarhus, Denmark.
